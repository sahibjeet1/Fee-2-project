Our Passion

Amoura, with its comforting environment, serene music and delicious delicacy, fosters a sense of tranquility amidst the hustle and bustle of daily life. Amoura makes hand-made, old-world European rustic bread and pastries seven days a week.

The unique flavours, textures and crust give our breads wonderful versatility and are a result of meticulous preparation using old-age artisan bread making techniques, premium ingredients and finest ovens. Coupled with perfectly brewed coffee and hot chocolate, Amoura offers a sensory experience more than just a culinary destination.
